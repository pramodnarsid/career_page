Who we are?

Rang De (RangDe.org) is an award winning, technology driven non-profit social enterprise that supports rural entrepreneurs with access to low cost credit through its web-based peer-to- peer crowdfunding platform. Since January 2008, Rang De has reached out to around 45,000 entrepreneurs across 17 states with more than 9,000 individuals across the world having become social investors to fight poverty in India so far.

Our Team

Rang De's team consists of professionals from diverse backgrounds with a strong sense of purpose to fight poverty in India. Content Writers - Impact

We are looking for writers who have a strong desire to make a difference in the lives of rural entrepreneurs.

We are looking for individuals who are keen to do written assignments seeking to increase awareness of Rang De’s activities and rural development issues.

You will work with over 50 volunteer writers who seek to write 100-word stories (“profiles”) about over 2,500 people every month.

Your areas of focus would be:

❯ Understanding the recruitment requirement of the organization
❯ Produce a minimum of 30-40 profiles a day operations

What you need to succeed:

❯ Bachelor's degree in Mass Communication, Mass Media & Communication Studies, English Literature or other related courses
❯ Good writing skills and words-per-minute count greater than 70
❯ Excellent Communication skills
❯ Experience of working with communities of writers

The position is based out of Bangalore and applicants from other cities should be willing to relocate. To apply for this job, please write to work@rangde.org with an updated CV and a brief note on why you think you are best suited for this role.
